# Cycling Template for Obsidian

This is a template for your cycling journalling in Obsidian. I will explain the template briefly.

First you will see a YAML format properties in checkbox style that will only accept two value: `true` and `false`. Then, for banner use "Pixel Banner" and customize the banner as per your preference.

After properties, you will see `{{date}}` and `{{time}}`. This will automatically create the date and time the moment you create this note.

Then, you will see a title for the days you will go cycling. It can be a specific days or daily or weekly or monthly or anything. Regular cycling is recommended.

Then there are some methods for improving your endurance for long term cycling. They are not a recommendation, you can replace them with what works for you.

If you are carrying a backpack, which you should for long rides. You can add more stuff to it, including the repair kits and etc.

Then comes data, finally. If you are using an app for tracking your cycling (like Strava), you will get this data from it. You can journal all of it here.

"Notes" section is quite important because it pushes you to think about your cycling ride and journal it. Maybe you saw an animal on the way, or someone crashed in front of you (or maybe it was you), or you saw beautiful scenery on the way.

Copy the template and paste it in `Templates/template.md` You can alter the template as per your preferences. Hope you enjoy. Check out my GitHub for more exciting stuff.
